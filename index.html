Amazing Adventure

Founded in 2022, Camp Enjoy Summer is a traditional and unique summer camp for children!

One of the best camps for children’s mental and physical development

The best supervisors for the safety and health of children

Located in the Mountains of Western North Carolina for 5 years, we emphasize outdoor activities such as hiking, kayaking, canoeing, sailing, mountain biking, and rock climbing different outdoor games (football basketball etc.), and also swimming. We believe in fun, adventure, safety, and great instruction.


We pay attention not only to the involvement of children in sports, but also to their Mental development. Classes in French and Spanish languages, mental arithmetic, and Rubik's cubes are also taught by teachers according to age.


There have also been a number of innovations in the camp over the last 2 years. For example, music and art are taught. Parents have noticed that such programs have had a positive change in the worldview and thinking of children in previous years. And their children are developing their interests and skills in the field of art.


If you would like to speak to someone about camp, please don’t hesitate to call us.
